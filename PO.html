Commission on Higher Education (CHED)

The CHED Merit Scholarship Program (CMSP) for the 2025–2026 academic year was open for incoming first-year college students, with an application deadline of June 20, 2025, according to several Facebook posts from May and June 2025. Benefits include up to ₱120,000 per academic year for private institutions or ₱80,000 for public institutions.

Department of Science and Technology (DOST)

The DOST Scholarship is a program by the Philippine Department of Science and Technology (DOST) and supports future scientists, engineers, and innovators through academic assistance and career opportunities.
